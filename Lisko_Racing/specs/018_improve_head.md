# Lizard Head Improvement

## Request
Tee liskon päästä parempi (Make the lizard's head better)

## Changes
- Updated the lizard head model:
    - **Composite Skull:** Replaced the simple cone head with a composite structure (Sphere skull + Cylinder snout + Cylinder jaw).
    - **Defined Features:** Added distinct nostrils on the snout tip.
    - **Enhanced Eyes:** Improved eye sockets (brow ridges) and added vertical slit pupils for a more reptile-like appearance.
    - **Jaw:** Added a lower jaw piece for depth.
- Maintained the overall realistic segmented structure.
